Welcome!

My name is Shen Fu (in Chinese: 傅燊). I graduated from Singapore Management University's Master of Philosophy in Economics Program. I was born in Xuancheng, Anhui in China and raised up there until I was 15. And then I finished my secondary education in Wugang No.3 High School in Wuhan. Later I did my undergraduate in CEMA, Central University of Finance and Economics (CUFE) and obtained a Master degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen). Now I'm doing PhD at School of Economics (SOE), Singapore Management University (SMU).

My research insterest is econometrics. Please feel free to contact me!
